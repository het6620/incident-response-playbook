# 🔄 Incident Response Lifecycle

Incident Response (IR) is a structured approach used to detect, contain, and recover from cybersecurity incidents.

## 🔥 What is a Cybersecurity Incident?
Any event that:
- Threatens confidentiality, integrity, or availability (CIA)
- Violates security policies
- Impacts business operations

### Examples:
- Ransomware attacks
- Unauthorized access
- Data breaches
- DDoS attacks

---

## ⚙️ IR Lifecycle Phases

### 1. Preparation
- Setup tools (SIEM, EDR)
- Define policies
- Train teams

### 2. Detection & Analysis
- Identify suspicious activity
- Analyze logs and alerts

### 3. Containment
- Isolate infected systems
- Block malicious traffic

### 4. Eradication
- Remove malware
- Patch vulnerabilities

### 5. Recovery
- Restore systems
- Monitor for reinfection

---

## 🎯 Importance of IR
- Reduces damage
- Ensures business continuity
- Supports legal compliance
- Improves security posture
